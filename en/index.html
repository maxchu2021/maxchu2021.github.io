VacayCar is a custom travel planning and online booking platform. The team included U.S. members, and the project required full English communication. The system was built using the Yii PHP Framework and supports itinerary browsing, custom trip planning, online booking, and a travel blog.

Taiwan Entomological Society

Developed an official website for the Taiwan Entomological Society using Drupal 10 content management framework, providing online donations, registration for annual meetings, paper submissions, and system export Word documents, as well as a backend content management system.

About

Hi, I'm Max, a Full-Stack Web Engineer with over 20 years of experience in web development. I specialize in React, Vue, and PHP (various MVC frameworks), and have a proven track record of building customized systems using Drupal.

Throughout my career, I have been deeply involved in diverse projects, including corporate websites, product systems, and platform development. My experience includes co-founding a startup with the AppWorks accelerator, where I contributed to the design and development of IoT products that were successfully launched on Amazon and the North American market. Additionally, I have extensive experience in cross-border collaboration, having worked with US-based teams to develop online travel booking and private car service platforms.

In practice, I thrive on solving complex technical challenges and architecting robust systems, with a constant focus on performance optimization and user experience. I enjoy exploring new domains and integrating various technologies into practical, high-impact solutions.

Currently, I work as a Freelancer, providing web development, system architecture, and technical consulting services. Whether you need to build a system "from zero to launch" or require a technical consultant to turn your ideas into a functional product, I am here to help. Feel free to reach out!
